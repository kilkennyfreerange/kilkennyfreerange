Place an Order — Kilkenny Free Range


Ducks are available all year round. Geese are reared primarily for the Christmas market from late November to New Years Eve.

People normally collect their products by appointment. The usual date for collection at Christmas is December 23rd. Due to the high demand at Christmas, it is advisable to place your order as early as possible.

If you're unsure what you need to order, feel free to contact us here. If you know what you want, go ahead and place an order using the form below!

Please note: Most birds will fall into the Medium weight range.

Home Delivery (Dublin)

We are offering an option of home delivery, currently available in Dublin only.

If you opt for Home Delivery you will need to be at home to receive your order. Deliveries to Dublin will take place on 22nd December. We will contact you to confirm.

An additional €7 charge will be added to orders for this delivery service.
